Strategy for sourcing

Sourcing refers to finding, interacting, and hiring the best match people for the business. It's a technique to showcase a successful image of the organisation and engage candidates through content, interaction, and strategic messaging. Hiring a skilled and qualified candidate is a crucial part of any organisation for growth. Quikdy follows a well-designed process to hire qualified candidates with the required skills.

Recruitment Process

The hiring process not only reflects the organisation's competence but also assists in attracting qualified candidates while reducing the time and money required for talent identification, attraction, engagement, recruitment, and retention.
“Quikdy hiring team quickly evaluates perfect candidates while maintaining focus on engaging the qualified candidates with a well-designed recruitment process.”

  1. Our Recruitment process starts with the identification of hiring needs by evaluating the job requirements, including the knowledge, skills, and experience required for the position.
  2. After identification of hiring needs, our team makes clear job descriptions including required skills, responsibility for the position, experience, qualification, and location
  3. The key components of the hiring process are finding the right talent and attracting and motivating them to apply. We use both internal and external sources of recruitment through advertisements job portals and social networking platforms.
  4. Now before moving to the interview process our experts narrow down the candidate list by screening the application based on qualification, experience, and skills and shortlisting the candidate who has all the potential.
  5. Now, before getting an offer letter or a rejection notice, the shortlisted candidates will go through the interview process including both face-to-face and virtual interviews
  6. After the successful interview and evaluation process candidate, our team conducts a detailed and engaging onboarding process and provides an offer letter including all data
